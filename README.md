#  Power BI Mobile Sales Dashboard  
Interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI | Data Visualization | Business Insights

This project is a fully interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze mobile sales across regions, brands, models, payment methods, and customer ratings.  
It provides a 360° view of business performance with clear KPIs, visual insights, and drill‑down capabilities.

---

##  Project Overview

The **Mobile Sales Dashboard** helps stakeholders understand:

- Which brands and models drive the most revenue  
- How sales vary across cities and regions  
- Customer satisfaction levels  
- Payment method preferences  
- Monthly and daily sales trends  
- Quantity, transactions, and average sales behavior  

The dashboard is designed for **business decision‑making**, **trend analysis**, and **performance monitoring**.

---

##  Key Features

###  KPI Cards  
- Total Sales  
- Total Quantity Sold  
- Total Transactions  
- Average Sales  

###  Visualizations  
- **Map View** — Sales by City  
- **Line Chart** — Quantity by Month  
- **Line Chart** — Sales by Day Name  
- **Bar Chart** — Customer Ratings  
- **Bar Chart** — Sales by Mobile Model  
- **Pie Chart** — Transactions by Payment Method  
- **Brand Performance Table**  

###  Slicers  
- Mobile Model  
- Payment Method  
- Brand  
- Day Name  

---

##  Insights Generated

###  Brand Performance  
- Apple leads in total sales, closely followed by Samsung  
- Xiaomi shows strong volume despite lower revenue  
- All brands have similar transaction counts  

###  Customer Behavior  
- Highest sales on Saturday, Monday, Friday  
- Strong preference for digital payments (UPI, Debit Card)  
- High customer satisfaction (majority 4–5 star ratings)  

###  Product Insights  
- iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, and Galaxy Note 20 are top sellers  
- Premium and mid‑range models dominate revenue  

###  Geographic Insights  
- Metro cities contribute the highest sales  
- Strong presence across India, Nepal, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka
